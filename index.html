how this works

we host recurring events in low-traffic spaces where you can spend time with others while enjoying yourself.

  • ✦ low cost to free: accessible by default
  • ✦ light structure for hosts: guidance that helps you feel like "you've got this".
  • ✦ a place to be yourself: no networking, please! just bring the "5-9" you :)
  • ✦ a respectful relationship with spaces: we gather responsibly and encourage support for local shops nearby

for guests (what you can expect)

you’ll probably like happy town if you…

  • want new friends but hate “networking energy”
  • like long walks and wandering conversations
  • miss the feeling of a friendly regular spot
  • enjoy gentle structure: prompts, activities, “you can join anytime” vibes

what you won’t have to do

  • pitch yourself
  • be extroverted on command
  • commit to a whole new identity
